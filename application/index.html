Applications

We are acutely attuned to the significance of each detail, and we craft our designs and surfaces with meticulous care. This attention to detail allows our products to find their perfect application in various domains, including automotive and caravans, interior design, and eco-friendly packaging solutions.

scroll

Wall Panelling and False Ceiling

RMD Boards leads the way in eco-friendly interior solutions, blending style and sustainability seamlessly. Our innovative materials, crafted with a commitment to environmental responsibility, redefine modern living spaces. With a range of high quality wood composites and low-impact finishes, RMD Boards sets a new standard for conscious interior design, creating spaces that harmonize with nature.

Container Houses

Container houses, designed for a lightweight and versatile living solution, find their perfect partner in RMD Board. These boards are not only lightweight but also waterproof, termite-proof, and chemical-resistant, making them an ideal material for all possible applications. With pre-decorated paneling, RMD Board enhances the functionality and aesthetics of container house interiors. Its excellent workability enables fast and easy fabrication of container houses, ensuring low maintenance and long-lasting durability for your container house.

Automobile

At RMD Boards, we offer green eco-friendly automotive solutions with our innovative trunk boards. Our commitment to sustainability and quality has revolutionized the industry. We specialize in creating high-quality interior boards that not only lightweight for automobiles but also contribute to a greener planet. Our trunk boards are lightweight, waterproof, and chemical-resistant, ensuring durability and low maintenance for the automotive industry while reducing our environmental footprint.

Packaging

At RMD Boards, eco-friendly packaging is at the forefront of our business. We prioritize both performance and the environment. Our boards are crafted from renewable and recycled materials, ensuring minimal ecological impact. By focusing on sustainability, we set a noteworthy standard for responsible manufacturing. Our commitment to green solutions reflects our vision for the future. We offer packaging solutions that are not only environmentally friendly but also durable and reliable, meeting the needs of businesses while reducing their carbon footprint.

Printing and Advertising

RMD Boards specializes in manufacturing top-quality advertising boards that boast a unique combination of features. Our boards are engineered to be strong and rigid, ensuring durability in various weather conditions. Crafted with a waterproof design, they stand up to the elements, maintaining their integrity even in the harshest environments. Remarkably lightweight, our boards offer easy installation and transport, making them a practical choice for diverse advertising needs. What sets RMD Boards apart is our commitment to sustainability; each board is further recyclable, contributing to a more eco-conscious approach in the industry.
