Working Today for a Sustainable Tomorrow

RMD Boards stands at the forefront of innovation in building and decorative materials, epitomizing the next generation of manufacturing. With an unwavering commitment to a sustainable future, we embark on a mission to safeguard our planet. By providing seamless access to a diverse range of sustainable products and spearheading breakthroughs in industrial recycling solutions, we set the standard for environmental stewardship in our industry.

About Us

Welcome to the world of RMD Boards, where we turn dreams into reality. We are a dynamic and innovative industrial group based in Dubai, UAE, guided by a philosophy of daring to dream. With RMD Boards, we've taken our first step toward realizing our global aspirations.

Our journey led us to diversify into GenNext materials for various sectors, including building and construction, interior decor, furniture, automobiles, marine, advertising, and more. All this is made possible at our state-of-the-art plant located in the Jebel Ali Free Zone, Dubai, UAE. We're committed to delivering cutting-edge solutions that transform industries, providing quality, innovation, and excellence with every product we offer.

Our Mission

At RMD Boards, our mission is to redefine interior design by creating innovative, sustainable wall panels that seamlessly combine environmental responsibility with personalized aesthetics. Our commitment is to minimize our ecological impact while empowering our customers to express their distinctive style through customizable coverings.

Through unwavering dedication to sustainability, quality, and creativity, we aspire to elevate living and working spaces, one wall panel at a time. We are driven by the vision of transforming interiors while protecting our planet's future, making a significant impact on both design and the environment.

Our Vision

At RMD Boards, we envision a future where sustainability harmoniously converges with artistry. We aspire to lead the world in sustainable wall panel solutions, creating interior spaces that authentically reflect the values and uniqueness of their occupants while safeguarding the health of our planet.

We are determined to be pioneers in innovation, continually pushing the limits of sustainable materials and design possibilities. Our vision extends to inspire a new era of environmentally conscious interior design, creating a timeless legacy of beauty, functionality, and sustainability for present and future generations to enjoy.

Our Values

Integrity: Upholding high ethical standards with honesty, transparency, and unwavering commitment to moral and ethical principles.
Excellence: Striving for excellence in all aspects, delivering top-quality products, and continually improving performance.
Ingenuity: Fostering creativity, innovation, and thinking outside the box to develop new solutions to meet customer needs.
Fairness: Treating all stakeholders with equity, fostering inclusivity and justice.
Customer Focus: Placing customers at the center, understanding their needs, and providing exceptional service to enhance satisfaction and loyalty.
Entrepreneurship: Encouraging a proactive, dynamic approach that drives innovation and growth within the organization.
